About this page

This page is a personal record of my mathematical study at Johns Hopkins University from September 2020 to December 2023. During this period, I was working, and I intentionally limited myself to at most one course each semester.

The goal was never to finish quickly. I wanted to cherish the opportunity to study mathematics and give each course the time and attention it deserved. I created this page to preserve that experience and to share a small collection of notes, reports, and academic records that remain meaningful to me.
